The project was a part of a semester long introduction to Object Oriented Programming using Java.

The concept of the game was relatively simple. The enemies were called Slicers which, when allowed to complete their path, dealt damage to the player. The player had to deploy defensive resources called towers which would deal damage to and kill the slicers.

Each tower and slicer have individual properties like damage dealt, cost, health, etc. The objective of the game was to survive multiple waves of slicers and finish the game with at least 1 life remaining.
