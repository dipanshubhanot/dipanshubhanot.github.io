The project was created in response to CodeBrew 2020, a hackathon with the theme of aiding health professionals in their fight against COVID-19. I participated in a team of 5 members, communicating remotely due to the government regulations.

The applications aimed to ease the transition of Victoria towards a state of Covid Normal. We aimed to do this by providing a simple digital mechanism to businesses and the authorities to easily identify and update test results for those tested for the virus.
